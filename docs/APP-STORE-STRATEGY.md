## App Store Name & Subtitle Strategy

Apple gives you two fields that are heavily indexed for search:
- **Title:** up to 30 characters
- **Subtitle:** up to 30 characters

Every unique word across title + subtitle + keywords field is indexed. Don't repeat words across these fields.

### Recommended Option

**Title:** `LumiMD: Doctor Visit Recorder`
*(29 characters — hits "doctor," "visit," "recorder")*

**Subtitle:** `AI Notes for Family Caregivers`
*(30 characters — hits "AI," "notes," "family," "caregivers")*

### Why This Works
- **"Doctor visit recorder"** matches the highest-intent search query for this category
- **"AI Notes"** positions against competitors and signals modern technology
- **"Family Caregivers"** directly targets your primary audience and differentiates from the competition (none of them use "caregiver" in their title/subtitle)
- Together they tell a complete story: *record doctor visits → get AI notes → share with family caregivers*

### Alternative Options

| Title | Subtitle | Trade-off |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| `LumiMD - Medical Visit Notes` | `Record & Share with Caregivers` | Broader "medical" keyword but less specific |
| `LumiMD: Record Doctor Visits` | `AI Summaries for Caregivers` | Action-oriented title, but "summaries" is longer |
| `LumiMD` | `Doctor Visit Recorder for Care` | Cleaner brand presence, but wastes title character space |

## Keywords Field (100 characters, comma-separated)

The keywords field is hidden from users but indexed for search. Rules: no spaces after commas, don't repeat words already in title/subtitle, singular forms only (Apple matches plural automatically).

```
medical,appointment,transcribe,medication,health,elderly,parent,aging,summary,share,track,remind,diagnosis,prescription,record
```

*(98 characters)*

**Keywords NOT included because they're already in title/subtitle:**
doctor, visit, recorder, AI, notes, family, caregiver

**High-value terms covered across all three fields:**
doctor, visit, recorder, AI, notes, family, caregiver, medical, appointment, transcribe, medication, health, elderly, parent, aging, summary, share, track, remind, diagnosis, prescription, record

## Screenshot Strategy

### Principles
- Lead with the caregiver value proposition (emotional hook)
- Show real UI, not abstract illustrations (builds trust for health apps)
- Use your brand cyan (#40C9D0) as the dominant background color
- Each screenshot = one clear message + one UI screen
- 6 screenshots recommended (Apple allows up to 10, but 6 is optimal)

### Screenshot Sequence

| # | Headline | Subtext | Screen Shown |
|---|----------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | **"Never Miss What Your Doctor Said"** | Record visits. Get AI-powered summaries. | Home dashboard with recent visit card |
| 2 | **"Every Detail, Organized"** | Diagnoses, medications, and next steps — extracted automatically. | Visit detail/summary screen |
| 3 | **"Keep Your Family in the Loop"** | A dedicated portal for caregivers — no app needed. | Caregiver web portal dashboard |
| 4 | **"Medication Safety, Built In"** | Drug interaction checks and refill tracking. | Medications screen with safety alert |
| 5 | **"Nothing Falls Through the Cracks"** | Action items and reminders from every visit. | Action items screen with checkboxes |
| 6 | **"Your Health Story, at a Glance"** | Track trends. Spot patterns. Stay informed. | Health metrics/trends screen |

### Design Specs
- **Device frame:** iPhone 15 Pro Max (1290 x 2796px)
- **Background:** Brand cyan gradient (#40C9D0 → #35A8AE)
- **Headline font:** Bold, white, large (Plus Jakarta Sans or SF Pro Display)
- **Subtext:** Regular weight, white with slight transparency
- **UI mockup:** Centered, with subtle shadow/depth, showing actual app screens
- **Safe area:** Keep text and critical elements away from top/bottom 150px
