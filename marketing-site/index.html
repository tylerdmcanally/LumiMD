Privacy & Security

Your health data belongs to you. Full stop.

We built LumiMD with the assumption that your health information is the most sensitive data you have. Every decision we make starts there.

End-to-end encryption

All visit audio and documents are encrypted before they leave your device. Data in transit and at rest is protected with AES-256.

Audio never stored

Audio is processed to create your visit notes, then permanently deleted within 24 hours. We keep only the notes. We never train AI models on your data.

You own your data

Export everything or delete your account at any time — no friction, no waiting period. Your data is yours, not ours to monetize.

Controlled caregiver access

You invite caregivers. You control what they see. Revoke access at any time. Caregivers get a read-only view — they can never modify your health records.

HIPAA-compliant

LumiMD is built to HIPAA standards. Your health information is never sold to insurers, advertisers, or data brokers. Ever.

US-based infrastructure

Your data is stored on US-based servers, subject to US data protection laws. No offshore processing of health records.

Privacy-first by design, not by policy

We don't just comply with privacy regulations — we design for privacy from the ground up. Visit audio is deleted, AI providers store nothing, caregiver access is explicit and revocable, and you have a full privacy audit log of every access event.

Frequently asked questions

Is it legal to use LumiMD during my doctor visits?

In most US states (39 out of 50), yes. These are "one-party consent" states — only one person (you) needs to consent.

However, 11 states require all parties to consent (California, Florida, Illinois, and others). Regardless of law, we strongly recommend letting your provider know you're using a note-taking app. Most are supportive when they understand it's for your personal health management. LumiMD detects your location and shows the applicable consent guidance before each visit.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. LumiMD uses end-to-end encryption for all data transmission and storage, is built to HIPAA standards, and uses enterprise-grade security throughout. Visit audio is processed and permanently deleted within 24 hours — we only keep your notes. We do not train AI models on your information, and we never sell data to any third party.

What is LumiBot and how does it work?

LumiBot is LumiMD's health companion — it doesn't just send reminders, it has context about your specific visits, diagnoses, and medications. After a new visit summary is ready, LumiBot offers a 3-step walkthrough: what was discussed, what medications changed, and what your action items are.

Between visits, it checks in on relevant health questions, nudges you on overdue action items, and helps you log vitals with trend context. LumiBot never gives clinical advice — it always points to your care team for anything medical.

How does caregiver sharing work?

From your settings, you invite a caregiver by email. They create their own LumiMD account, accept the invite, and get a read-only view of your health data. You control exactly what they can see, and you can revoke access at any time.

Caregivers can use the LumiMD mobile app (with a caregiver role) or the Caregiver Portal at portal.lumimd.app for a full multi-patient dashboard experience. They never have the ability to edit or delete your health records.

Can I upload paperwork instead of using the app during my visit?

Yes — this is one of our most-used features. Photograph your after-visit summary (up to 10 pages) or upload a PDF from your patient portal, and LumiMD extracts the same structured data as an in-app visit: diagnoses, medications, action items, and follow-up instructions. If you both used the app and received paperwork from the same visit, LumiMD merges them automatically.

Does it work for telehealth or phone appointments?

Yes. For telehealth visits, you can use LumiMD on the same device or have it listen nearby. It works just as well for virtual visits. Alternatively, if your provider sends an after-visit summary, just upload the document instead.

Is LumiMD free?

LumiMD is free to use during the beta period. The app, caregiver sharing, medication reminders, LumiBot, and the caregiver portal are all included. We'll share pricing plans before the beta ends — and early users will receive favorable terms as a thank-you for helping us build the product.
