A Personal Health Assistant for Patient Engagement

LumiMD helps patients capture, understand, and act on their healthcare information. Built by a PA who saw firsthand how much gets lost between appointments.

Core Functionality

Visit Recording Mobile

Patients record appointments in-app. Audio is transcribed and processed using AI to generate structured visit summaries.

AI-Generated Summaries Mobile + Web

Each visit produces a summary with diagnosis, medications started/stopped, and actionable follow-up items.

Medication Management Mobile + Web

Medications auto-sync from visit summaries. Smart reminders fire based on prescribed frequency and timing.

Caregiver Access Web Portal

Patients can securely share records with family members or caregivers via the web portal for coordinated care.
